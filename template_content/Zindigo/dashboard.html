Branding Corner

What first inspired Kara Ross to start creating bold & unique jewelery?

To commemorate a safari to Africa with her parents at the age of thirteen, Kara’s parents allowed her to choose a native gemstone of her choice. When they returned to their home, Kara was told she could design andcreate a ring with the tourmaline she chose. Kara decided to create a square cut ring on an 18 karat gold band with diamonds. “To know that I could create something wholly original at a young age really affected me.”
